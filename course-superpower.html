The Symptom

Living in high-pressure environments means the whole family might be soaked in Cortisol. This looks like short tempers, constant arguing, or shutting down (the Freeze).

It's not attitude. It's Hypervigilance.

Children have Mirror Neurons that are always watching. They don't do what you say; they do what you do. If you react to stress with shouting, their brain "copy-pastes" that behavior.
